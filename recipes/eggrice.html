Creating your eggrice:

This recipe will allow you to eat eggrice all day every day and it will make you super cute and healthy, guarenteed!

Ingredients:

  • White rice
  • Egg
  • Green onion
  • Sesame oil
  • Soy sauce

Steps:

  1. Cook rice as per packet instructions
  2. Once rice is cooked, distribute a teaspoon of sesame oil throughout
  3. Fry one egg on high heat so all the white is set but yolk is still runny
  4. Deposit egg on top of rice
  5. Garnish with finely chopped green onion and soy sauce
